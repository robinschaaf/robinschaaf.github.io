I'm a long-time creator - I started with fashion and jewelry design in high school and college, on to being a web and database developer after college. And, depending on how well the UCD went, hopefully little need to retool later. I love the challenge of a good problem, and the challenge of beauty and simplicity. And in building software products that delight and work like second nature.
